about

kristen iemma is a PhD student in American Studies and an MA candidate in Public Humanities at Brown University, and holds an MSLIS from Pratt Institute. Her research addresses institutional record keeping practices and attendant questions of representation, authority, and memory transmission. Her projects have also engaged with issues of information justice, digital rights, and privacy.
